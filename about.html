Hillzone Group is a collective association of investment professionals who seek buyout and growth equity investments in lower middle market businesses with exceptional management teams. An experienced team of 30 investment professionals who, on behalf of a highly seasoned group of institutional investors, collectively manage approximately $5 billion in assets, including some of the largest pension funds, insurance firms, and endowments.

Since our inception, our leadership team has been with the organization and all our investors have been investing together for over 9 years. We believe there is no substitute for the proven experience that comes from successfully investing in hundreds of businesses through varying economic cycles. It is this experience that forms the backbone of our expertise and positions us to add significant value to every company and management team that we support.

Our team has a singular focus on and significant investing experience in: Investments & Corporate Banking, Private Wealth Management, Direct Investments. Over 11 years, Hillzone's investment team has sourced and led hundreds of transactions in these core industries. Our deep understanding of these sectors has enabled us to be successful over time and to stay ahead of changing trends.
